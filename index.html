Authentic Tastes of the South, Served Fresh

Masala Dosa

Origin: Karnataka

A crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potato mash. Served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves

Best Places: Vidyarthi Bhavan, CTR (Malleshwaram), MTR

Idli

Origin: Tamil Nadu

Steamed rice cakes, soft and fluffy, typically eaten with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, salt

Best Places: Brahmin's Coffee Bar, Veena Stores

Vada

Origin: Tamil Nadu

Savory deep-fried lentil doughnuts, crispy outside and soft inside.

Ingredients: Urad dal, spices, green chili, curry leaves

Best Places: SLV Corner, New Krishna Bhavan

Appam

Origin: Kerala

Soft-centered hoppers with crispy edges, typically served with coconut milk or stew.

Ingredients: Rice, coconut milk, yeast

Best Places: Kerala Pavilion, Kumarakom Restaurant

Pesarattu

Origin: Andhra Pradesh

Green gram-based dosa, often eaten with ginger chutney.

Ingredients: Green gram, ginger, green chilies, cumin

Best Places: Chutneys Restaurant, Simply South

Hyderabadi Biryani

Origin: Telangana

Fragrant rice layered with marinated meat and spices, cooked using dum method.

Ingredients: Basmati rice, chicken/mutton, saffron, spices

Best Places: Meghana Foods, Paradise Biryani
