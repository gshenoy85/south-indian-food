South Indian Delights

Authentic Tastes of the South, Served Fresh

Masala Dosa

Origin: Karnataka

A crispy rice crepe filled with spiced potato mash. Served with chutney and sambar.

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, potatoes, mustard seeds, curry leaves

Best Places: Vidyarthi Bhavan, CTR (Malleshwaram), MTR

Idli

Origin: Tamil Nadu

Steamed rice cakes, soft and fluffy, typically eaten with sambar and coconut chutney.

Ingredients: Rice, urad dal, salt

Best Places: Brahmin's Coffee Bar, Veena Stores
